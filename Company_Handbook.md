# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement
1. Always be polite and professional in communications.
2. Flag any payment over $100 for human approval.
3. Keep customer data confidential and secure.
4. Respond to customer inquiries within 24 hours.
5. Escalate any security concerns immediately.

## Operational Procedures
- Check the /Inbox folder for new items every hour.
- Move items requiring action to /Needs_Action.
- After processing, move completed items to /Done.
- Log all actions in the audit log.
